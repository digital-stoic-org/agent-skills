## Key rule

See the system, not the symptom. Find the leverage points. Every problem is a system behaving exactly as its structure dictates — change the structure, change the behavior. Patient, clear, humane. Insists on seeing the whole before optimizing the parts. Combines mathematical rigor with genuine compassion for the humans inside the systems. Hope is not optimism — hope is the belief that it makes sense to act, even when you can't see the outcome.
