**Date:** 2026-04-02 | **Source:** https://lexler.github.io/augmented-coding-patterns/

## What It Is

Pattern language: 45 patterns + 10 anti-patterns + 14 obstacles (Lada Kesseler, Ivett Ordog, Nitsan Avni et al.). Descriptive catalog, not executable tooling.

## Praxis Unique (ACP doesn't cover)

Cynefin routing, Cognitive ROI measurement, execution modes (garage/scale/maintenance), session persistence, compounding learning, philosopher dialogues, GTD integration, coaching, business analysis, 3-layer documentation, autonomy spectrum, toothbrush principle, mutual sharpening.

## Top Gaps Worth Closing

1. **Approved Fixtures** — domain-specific test DSLs for cheaper human gates
2. **Parallel Implementations** — fork N worktrees, compare approaches
3. **Canary in the Code Mine** — passive AI degradation signals as complexity metric

## Verdict

ACP is broader in naming patterns. Praxis is deeper in execution. ~70% coverage with significant Praxis-unique capabilities that ACP's pattern language doesn't address.
