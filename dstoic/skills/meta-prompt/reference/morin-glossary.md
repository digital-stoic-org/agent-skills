# Morin Glossary — *pensée complexe* behind RELIANCE

Teaching / onboarding material. NOT loaded into context during the loop — read this when
explaining RELIANCE to a human, or when you need the conceptual *why* behind a zone.

The vocabulary comes from Edgar Morin's *La Méthode* and *Introduction à la pensée complexe*.
The point of borrowing it: a meta-prompt for complex work should itself resist linear,
reductionist framing. Navigation metaphors (heading, compass, north star) smuggle in
single-trajectory thinking — exactly what Morin spent a career dismantling. These terms name
the **recursive, dialogic, environment-coupled** shape the tool actually has.

## Provenance — honest labelling

| Term | Genuinely Morin? | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **RELIANCE** | ✅ yes | Morin adopted it (from Marcel Bolle de Bal). Core to *La Méthode*: *relier* what reductionism separates. |
| **FINALITÉ** | ✅ yes | Used throughout — the orienting *why* of a system, emergent, non-mechanical. Distinct from a goal-with-fixed-steps. |
| **BOUCLE** (récursive) | ✅ yes | The recursive loop where output re-enters as input — Morin's *boucle récursive*. |
| **DIALOGIQUE** | ✅ yes | Holding antagonistic-yet-complementary demands together without resolving. |
| **ÉCO** (auto-éco-organisation) | ✅ yes | A system coupled to its milieu, re-organising as the milieu shifts. |
| **PROJET** | 🟡 resonant | Plain French "aim/project"; used loosely, not a sharp Morin term. Honest as a revisable-intent label. |
| **MAIN** | ❌ no | Human-in-the-loop primitive. Labelled as such — no false attribution. |
| **RAIL** | ❌ no | An acronym (Role · Aim+bounds · Inputs · Landed). Connotes guidance-without-dictating-the-path. |

Do not present MAIN or RAIL as Morin concepts. The honesty is the point — mislabelling a
metaphor as terminology is exactly the reductionism the tool rejects.

## The concepts, in plain language

### RELIANCE — *re-link what division separates*
Reductionism understands by cutting things apart. Morin's counter-move: the whole exists only
in the *links*. In the file, this is literal — the zones don't mean anything alone; the value
lives in their cross-signalling (LOOP warns DIALOGIC, ÉCO feeds LOOP, MAIN overrides all). The
file is named after the principle because fusing the streams into one place IS reliance.

### FINALITÉ — *the orienting why (≠ a goal-with-steps)*
A finalité directs a system without scripting its path. This is precisely why RELIANCE is *not*
an autonomous `/goal`: a finalité orients, it doesn't mechanise. The whole run orbits this fixed
point; everything below it stays revisable. (See the `// future` note on per-RAIL autonomy: a
sharp finalité + machine-checkable LANDED is what an autonomous loop would need — RELIANCE keeps
the human gate in, making it the human-in-the-loop *superset* of /goal.)

### BOUCLE — *output becomes input*
The recursive loop: what happened feeds what follows. The LOOP zone is the persisted memory of
this — append-only, re-read every session. Its second stream (🤖 COLLAB: how the AI worked) is
what makes the loop *intelligent* rather than a changelog: the next session adjusts its own
behaviour from observed successes/failures/drift. Recursing on observed AI behaviour as a
first-class, fed-forward stream is the genuinely novel part of this design.

### DIALOGIQUE — *hold the contradiction, don't resolve it*
A compass converges to one bearing (linear). Dialogic keeps opposing demands live ("ship fast ↔
ship right") and lets the tension generate the next move. As an AI instruction this is better
than "check against true north": it tells the model to **resist premature convergence** and the
"you're absolutely right" reflex. The challenge zone is dialogic, not corrective.

### ÉCO — *coupled to the milieu, re-organise on drift*
Auto-éco-organisation: the work isn't free-floating, it's coupled to its environment (the hot
files). When the milieu shifts under the loop, the loop notices (drift detection) and
re-organises (back-sync). The ref/wip/park mutability tiers are the drift-check budget — cheap
for frozen files, full for live ones, skipped for dormant.

## Why this is worth the jargon

For a tool taught from at an AI school: the meta-prompt smuggles in *pensée complexe* as the
native vocabulary of working with AI. The adopter learns a real thinking framework as a
by-product of using the ledger. Each term carries a one-line gloss in the legend, so the cost of
the jargon is one line, and the payoff is a non-reductionist mental model. That trade is
deliberate — and it's the reliance principle applied to the documentation itself: name linked to
meaning linked to function, never left abstract.
